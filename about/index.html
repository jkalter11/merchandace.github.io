About Merchandace

We’re building the biggest collection of high-quality asexual merchandise on the web.

There’s a huge amount of asexual merchandise out there on the web, but it can be hard to find. That’s why we created Merchandace - a catalogue of all the best merchandise to help you show off your pride!

We’ve partnered with Zazzle, CafePress, RedBubble, Look Human, Amazon and Etsy to bring you loads of fantastic ace products all in one place. Discover asexy T-shirts, accessories, badges, coffee mugs, bags and so much more - all in one convenient and easy-to-browse website.

Merchandace launched on 1st October 2015 with a catalogue of 380 different products, and we’re adding more and more every day. If you’ve spotted something that we’re missing, please do let us know about it, and we’ll be sure to add it to the catalogue.

Merchandace also exists to support asexual and LGBTQ creators: the majority of the products in our catalogue are created by people just like you - either by using a print-on-demand service like Zazzle, or by handmaking and selling their own items on Etsy. Each creator gets to set their own prices and thus their own profit levels, and by purchasing these products you’re supporting their work and giving them the encouragement they need to keep creating great products.

If you’re a budding designer and would like to create your own ace merchandise, but don’t know how, we’re more than happy to help out. We can help you set up your accounts and upload your designs, and then we’ll list you in Merchandace to boost your exposure. If you’d like to find out more, head over to the Get Involved page!

Although Merchandace is provided as a free service to the community, running it and keeping it up to date does have associated costs. For that reason, we use affiliate links to connect you to the various products. This allows us to receive a small referral fee from the stores, which we use to keep this site running - and as it’s paid fully by the stores, it doesn’t affect the total that you pay, nor the royalties received by the designers for their work, so everyone wins.

We hope that you enjoy browsing Merchandace. If you’d like us to keep you updated with all the latest products and offers, please like us on Facebook, and follow us on Twitter and Tumblr. We’ll be posting regular updates on there to let you know what’s going on. We’d also love to hear your feedback - please get in touch!
