Asexualise

Asexualise is run by Sandra Bellamy - a Heteroromantic, Grey A, Asexual, Younger Cougar, who doesn't like sex, just kissing. Her mission is to get asexuality recognized as a sexual orientation it its own right throughout the globe, so that asexuals never have to live in fear of ridicule ever again. She likes to give asexuals a voice, to empower and enhance our asexual life through her Asexualise brand, and to educate others.
