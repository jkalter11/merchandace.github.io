Asexual Awareness Week

Asexual Awareness Week began in 2010 as a campaign targeting the LGBT community and its leadership for greater awareness. California activist Sara Beth Brooks worked with AVEN founder David Jay to plan the first year which was primarily conducted online through blogs and web visibility. The Week is now a major fixture in the international asexual calendar every October.
