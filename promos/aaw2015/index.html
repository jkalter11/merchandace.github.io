Asexual Awareness Week 2015 is in the cards

Get ready for the premier awareness event in the asexual calendar with a selection of hand-picked products from Merchandace!

Asexual Awareness Week is possibly the most important time of year for aces. It’s when we make our voices heard and let the world know that yes, we do exist. To help you with this, we at Merchandace have selected the very best products to help you show your pride and let everyone know about our orientation!

Offical Asexual Awareness Week merch

Where better to start than with the stuff the guys at AAW themselves have made? Choose from T-shirts with slogans like “Ace and Proud”, “Some People Are Asexual, Get Over It”, and “I Am An Ally” - or how about a sticker with the Asexual Awareness Week bubble logo?
