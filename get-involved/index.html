There are many ways you can get involved with Merchandace!

Modelling / Photography

We’d love to see your photos of you and your friends modelling your favourite asexual merchandise - and we’ll even feature the best ones on the site! What better way to show your pride? Please get in touch!

Selling your own merchandise

Merchandace is always on the lookout for high-quality asexual merchandise to add to our catalogue! If you’ve designed something, or spotted something that we’ve missed, please let us know about it, and we’ll add it to the site.

If you’re a budding designer, but don’t know where to start with getting your stuff online and into the hands of your customers, we’re always happy to help. We can get you set up with a print-on-demand supplier such as Zazzle, or help you list your handicrafts on Etsy or Amazon. We’ll then list them on Merchandace to help expose you to a wider audience. There aren’t usually any up-front costs involved with this: the stores only take a fee out of what you sell, and of course we won’t charge you a penny!

Technical Work

For the more technically inclined, we’re always happy to accept improvements to our design or new product listings. The site’s run on an offline Python backend, rendered to a static site using Jekyll and hosted using GitHub Pages. Do get in touch if you’d like to help out!
