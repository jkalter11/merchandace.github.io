Get Involved

There are many ways you can get involved with Merchandace!

Modelling / Photography

We’d love to see your photos of yourself and your friends modelling your favourite asexual merchandise - and we’ll even feature the best ones on the site! What better way to show your pride? Please get in touch!

Selling your own merchandise

Merchandace is always on the lookout for high-quality asexual merchandise to add to our catalogue! If you’ve designed something, please let us know about it, and we’ll add it to the site.

If you’ve designed something but don’t know where to start with getting it online and in the hands of your customers, we’re always happy to help. We can get you set up with an on-demand supplier such as Zazzle or CafePress, or help you list your handicrafts on Etsy or Amazon. This generally doesn’t involve any up-front costs - we won’t charge you anything, and naturally you get to set and keep your royalties.

Technical Work

For the more technically inclined, we’re always happy to accept improvements to our design or new product listings. The site’s built in Jekyll and hosted using GitHub Pages - feel free to fork the repo and send us some pull requests!
